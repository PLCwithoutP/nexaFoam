## [1.0.0] - 2025-09-24

### Added
- [ ] Vibrational temperature field (TVib) is implemented.
- [ ] JANAF thermodynamics expanded to 9-coefficient polynomials.
- [ ] Blottner–Eucken model for viscosity and thermal conductivity is implemented.
- [ ] Internal energies are implemented individually (mode-resolved).
- [ ] Specific heat constants are implemented for each energy mode.
- [ ] Vibrational energy check for atomic vs. molecular gases is implemented.
- [ ] Project compiles and works in the given format.
